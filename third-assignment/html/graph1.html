GRAPH I - CHOROPLET MAP (TREES ABUNDANCE)

Choropleth maps provide an easy way to visualize how a variable changes across a geographic area, meaning it shows the level of variability within a region. In this case the geographic area considered is Trento and the map plots the tree abundance per neighborhood with a range of colors that goes from white to dark green: the darker it is, the more trees there are in that specific neighborhood. Finally, a tooltip provides more specific information such as the neighborhood's name, its tree abundance and the area covered by it.

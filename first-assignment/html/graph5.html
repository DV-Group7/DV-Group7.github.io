GRAPH V - WAFFLE CHARTS

Multiple "Waffle Chart" type graphs are shown, each representing a neighborhood, where only the top 5 most prevalent tree species in the Trento area are considered.
For each chart, the various species are differentiated according to their percentage, allowing comparison of the spread of each species in each neighborhood.
NB! The 'Sardagna' district was excluded from the representations as it does not contain any of the trees considered below.

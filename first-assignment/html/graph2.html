GRAPH II - HORIZONTAL STACKED BARCHART

The following "Horizontal Stacked Bar Chart" highlights the total number of trees in each neighborhood. In particular, it differentiates for each neighborhood the amounts of trees that belong to the top 5 most common species in the Trento area from the remaining species that are grouped together in a single category.
We find on the y-axis the names of the neighborhoods and on the x-axis the number of trees.

GRAPH II - RADAR CHART

A Radar Chart is a way of showing multiple data points and the variation between them. They are often useful for comparing the points of two or more different data sets.
Considering the municipality of Salorno the following Radar Chart plots, for each year among 2021, 2017, 2013, 2009, 2005, 2001, 1997 and 1993, the mean temperature of every month.

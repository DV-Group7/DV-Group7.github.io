GRAPH III - RIDGELINE CHART

A Ridgeline plot shows the distribution of a numeric value for several groups.
Considering the municipality of Salorno, you can choose one year among 2021, 2017, 2013, 2009, 2005, 2001, 1997 and 1993. For each year there are 12 different Ridgeline plots, each one representing a single month. In particular, each of them, plot the Ridgeline for minimum and maximum daily temperature of the considered month. On the x axis we have the temperature (in degrees Celsius) while on the y axis, the frequency.

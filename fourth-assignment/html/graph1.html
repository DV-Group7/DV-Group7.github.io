GRAPH I - LINE CHART

A Line Chart is a way of plotting data points on a line. Often, it is used to show trend data, or the comparison of two data sets. Considering the municipality of Salorno and the following years: 2021, 2017, 2013, 2009, 2005, 2001, 1997 and 1993, the line-chart plot below, represents for each year two lines that indicate the minimum and maximum temperature of each month of the considered year and a scatter plot of the mean temperature of that year. In particular, the x axis contains the 12 months of the year, while on the y axis we have the temperature in degrees Celsius. Finally it has been chosen one color per year and for each of them, one darker shade for the minimum temperature and one brighter shade for the maximum temperature.
